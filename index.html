climbbook.de is your social media climbing portal!
We want to offer you the biggest route database of the world. With complete social media integration, all your friends know your newest climbs. It is possible to add routes, create a topo, add ascents to routes and share your ascents on facebook, twitter or google+.
Furthermore we offer a huge feature set for climbing hall owners. So you can add all your routes to climbbook and enjoy the features like: High scores, QR-Code printing, topo editor, publicity and so on.

Wow!!

You are standing in the city and you see this perfect route. You pick up your phone, start the climbbook app and take a picture. The app takes your geolocations and uploads the route and BAM! a new spot is born. Next time you pick your friends and a crashpad and go out to discover this new world of bouldering. Other climbers of your town can participate as well!
